PULSE PROTOCOL

UX Design Internship

Timeline: May 2020 - August 2020
My Role: Product Designer
Design Tools: Figma
Team: Megan Chang, Iryna Kulyk, Theo Skye (Head of Product), Robert Page (CEO)

Context.

PulseProtocol is a platform that connects patients with doctors around the world, allowing patients to get the care that they need. Pulse has three user groups that use the platform in distinct ways:

Patients Patients use Pulse to gain access to medical care from doctors in countries other than their own. Doctors Doctors use Pulse to gain access to a network of international patients. Facilitators Facilitators are the bridge between doctors and patients, and assist patients in planning and preparing for their medical travel.

As a UX design intern for Pulse, I was tasked with designing home and landing pages to generate additional leads from these user groups. I also was tasked with developing a new Accounting & Analytics Dashboard and Task Management System for use by doctors and facilitators in order to increase efficiency and ease of use of the platform.
